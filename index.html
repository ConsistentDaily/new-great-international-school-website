GREAT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Great International School (GIS) is a private school located near Hoskote, Karnataka, India. The school offers a CBSE curriculum from pre-school to Grade 10, with a focus on holistic education and the development of the whole child. Courses We Offer

Great International School (GIS) is a private school located near Hoskote, Karnataka, India. The school follows an CBSE & NCRT (State Board) based curriculum, from grade 1 to 8. Grades 9 and 10 follow the Karnataka state board, SSLC syllabus.

1. KINDERGARTEN & PRIMARY SCHOOL.

Our kindergarten and Primary school program commenced in the year 2008, we have a decade and plus experience in the kindergarten field. The kindergartensection is classified into nursery, LKG (lower kindergarten), and UKG (upper kindergarten). We take children from 2.3 years of age.

2. MIDDLE SCHOOL.

Middle school consists of grade six, seven, and eight. We follow CBSE & NCERT curriculum. It is a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses a holistic approach, focusing on academical, practical, and analytical knowledge.

3. HIGH SCHOOL.

The high school sections are grades 9 and 10,these grades follow the Karnataka SSLC syllabus. The books and curriculum are revised and provided by the Karnataka board.

About Us

Welcome to Great International School ! We're a vibrant community of learners committed to providing an exceptional education for young students.

Our Belief is to "RISE. LEARN. SHINE”.

  • TO RISE is to take the first step. Showing up and being there is the first step to achieve anything. Just like the sun rises every day, no matter what. We also need to rise, no matter howthe day feels like.
  • TO LEARN is everything that happens in between. This is probably the most important aspect of life. We are all life long learners. What we learn and how we learn is what determines the success
  • TO SHINE is to bring out our hidden gems. To bring out our best selves, no matter what. Ready to accept challenges and shine to a better version of ourselves every day. To shine internally so that it radiates outside.
  • OUR VISION & OUR MISSION To prepare our child by developing the necessary skills within themto pursue their aspirations, and contribute positively to the world. Along, byinstilling core values and morals, that helps them in life.

    Our mission is to provide a safe haven where everyone is valued and respected. All staff members, in partnership with parents and families are fully committed to students’ college and career readiness. Students are empowered to meet current and future challenges to develop social awareness, civic responsibility, and personal growth.
