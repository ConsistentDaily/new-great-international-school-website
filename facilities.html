GREAT INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Great International School (GIS) is a private school located near Hoskote, Karnataka, India. The school offers a CBSE curriculum from pre-school to Grade 10, with a focus on holistic education and the development of the whole child. GIS emphasizes academics, extracurricular activities, and character development, aiming to prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.
